About

Kara Yoo Materials Blog

Kara Yoo Jewelry began in 2012 with the mission to create versatile and thoughtfully crafted pieces for women of all ages. All designs are conceptualized by Kara and hand-crafted in-house, in the beautiful city of Vancouver, Canada. Kara Yoo Jewelry is made to last, using quality materials such as tarnish-resistant Argentium Sterling Silver, Gold-Fill and solid Gold. Kara Yoo is stocked in over 35 boutiques worldwide.

Coming from a family of entrepreneurs, Kara was inspired to create a business that was true to herself and her passions. Drawing inspiration from fashion, architecture and nature, Kara's pieces focus on crafting an experience that strives to redefine the everyday classic.
