Numbered Lists

Sample Code:

<ol>
<li>Always store your jewelry in a dry place and separate from other jewelry which could scratch your piece. Cloth pouches or resealable baggies work great.</li>
<li>To minimize tarnish, store your jewelry in an airtight container such as a resealable baggie.</li>
<li>Use a lint-free jewelry polish cloth and gently rub to remove any tarnish or restore shine. Avoid polishing plated items.</li>
<li>Remove all jewelry before taking a shower or using any harsh chemicals such as perfumes, chlorine, hairspray etc. which can cause a dullness in appearance or damage the metal.</li>
</ol>

Example:

  1. Always store your jewelry in a dry place and separate from other jewelry which could scratch your piece. Cloth pouches or resealable baggies work great.
  2. To minimize tarnish, store your jewelry in an airtight container such as a resealable baggie.
  3. Use a lint-free jewelry polish cloth and gently rub to remove any tarnish or restore shine. Avoid polishing plated items.
  4. Remove all jewelry before taking a shower or using any harsh chemicals such as perfumes, chlorine, hairspray etc. which can cause a dullness in appearance or damage the metal.
